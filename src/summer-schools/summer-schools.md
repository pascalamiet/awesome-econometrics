# Summer Schools in Econometrics, Statistics & Machine Learning

Upcoming and recurring summer schools for PhD students and early-career researchers. Organized by field; dates refer to the 2026 edition unless noted.

## Econometrics & Applied Economics

- [NBER Summer Institute](https://www.nber.org/conferences/summer-institute-2026)
  - **Organizer**: National Bureau of Economic Research
  - **Dates**: July 13–31, 2026
  - **Location**: Cambridge, MA, USA
  - **Topics**: Applied econometrics, labor, health, finance, macro, trade; includes a dedicated *Frontier Econometric Methods* session (July 30, org. Andrews, Athey & Walters)

- [CEMFI Summer School](https://www.cemfi.es/programs/css/index.asp)
  - **Organizer**: Centro de Estudios Monetarios y Financieros (CEMFI)
  - **Dates**: August 17 – September 4, 2026
  - **Location**: Madrid, Spain
  - **Topics**: One-week intensive courses for practitioners, central bankers, and academics; 2026 courses include Difference-in-Differences with Panel Data (Wooldridge), Data Science for Economics (Rauh), Dynamics & Heterogeneity in Panel Data (Bonhomme), High-Frequency Event Studies (Swanson), AI for Economic Measurement (Kazinnik)

- [Barcelona GSE Summer School — Microeconometrics](https://bse.eu/summer-school/microeconometrics)
  - **Organizer**: Barcelona School of Economics
  - **Dates**: June 22 – July 17, 2026
  - **Location**: Barcelona, Spain
  - **Topics**: Microeconometric techniques, qualitative variables, sample selection, censoring, panel data

- [Barcelona GSE Summer School — Macroeconometrics](https://bse.eu/summer-school/macroeconometrics)
  - **Organizer**: Barcelona School of Economics
  - **Dates**: June 22 – July 17, 2026
  - **Location**: Barcelona, Spain
  - **Topics**: Time series methods, VAR, empirical macroeconomics, Bayesian macroeconometrics

- [DSE Summer School — Dynamic Structural Econometrics](https://dseconf.org/dse2026)
  - **Organizer**: Econometric Society
  - **Dates**: August 3–8, 2026
  - **Location**: University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA
  - **Topics**: Dynamic finance and banking, structural econometrics, computational methods

- [Asian Summer School in Econometrics and Statistics](https://www.econometricsociety.org/regional-activities/schedule/2026/07/13/2026-Asian-Summer-School-in-Econometrics-and-Statistics)
  - **Organizer**: Econometric Society
  - **Dates**: July 13–19, 2026
  - **Location**: Beijing, China
  - **Topics**: Econometric theory, statistics, recent methodological advances

- [Africa Summer School in Economics (AFSS-2026)](https://www.econometricsociety.org/regional-activities/schedule/2026/06/05/2026-Africa-Summer-School-in-Economics-AFSS-2026-Hybrid)
  - **Organizer**: Econometric Society
  - **Dates**: June 5–9, 2026 (hybrid)
  - **Location**: American University in Cairo, Egypt
  - **Topics**: Econometrics, applied economics

- [IZA@LISER Summer School in Labor Economics](https://www.liser.lu/events/SummerSchool2026)
  - **Organizer**: IZA Institute of Labor Economics & LISER
  - **Dates**: June 1–3, 2026
  - **Location**: Remich, Luxembourg
  - **Topics**: Labor economics, applied empirical methods; for PhD students working on labor and related topics

- [SoFiE Financial Econometrics Summer School](https://www.stern.nyu.edu/experience-stern/about/departments-centers-initiatives/centers-of-research/volatility-and-risk-institute/sofie/sofie-events/sofie-schools)

  - **Dates**: July 6–10, 2026
  - **Location**: NYU Shanghai, China

- [CEPR Summer School — International and Development Economics](https://cepr.org/events/25th-summer-school-international-and-development-economics)

  - **Location**: Castello di Gargonza, Tuscany, Italy

- [Essex Summer School in Social Science Data Analysis](https://essexsummerschool.com/)

- [Global School in Empirical Research Methods (GSERM) — Causal Inference](https://www.summerschoolsineurope.eu/course/causal-inference-4/)
  - **Organizer**: University of St. Gallen
  - **Dates**: June 22–26, 2026
  - **Location**: St. Gallen, Switzerland
  - **Topics**: Causal inference, quasi-experimental methods; 4 ECTS credits


## Statistics & Bayesian Methods

  - **Location**: Cambridge, UK (Department of Engineering & Pembroke College)

  - **Location**: Nagoya, Japan
