# Causal Inference Courses

The following is a list of free courses in Causal Inference, sorted by format and date.

- [Video Lectures](#video-lectures)
- [Slides](#slides)
- [Notes](#notes)



## Video Lectures

- [Introduction to Causal Inference](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoazKTcS0RzZ1SUgeOgc6SWt51gfT80N0)
  - **Author**: Brady Neal (Mila / McGill)
  - **Year**: 2020
  - **Lectures**: 12 x 1h:00 [[Material](https://www.bradyneal.com/causal-inference-course)]
  - **Topics**: Potential Outcomes, Graphical Models, Backdoor & Frontdoor Adjustment, do-calculus, RCTs, CATE Estimation, Sensitivity Analysis

- [The Effect: Videos on Causal Inference](https://www.youtube.com/c/NickHuntingtonKlein)
  - **Author**: Nick Huntington-Klein (Seattle University)
  - **Year**: 2022
  - **Lectures**: 43 x 0h:20 [[Material](https://theeffectbook.net/)]
  - **Topics**: Research Design, DAGs, Matching, Event Studies, Differences-in-Differences, Regression Discontinuity, Instrumental Variables

## Slides

- [Machine Learning & Causal Inference](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/faculty-research/labs-initiatives/sil/research/methods/ai-machine-learning/short-course)
  - **Author**: Susan Athey & Guido Imbens (Stanford GSB)
  - **Year**: 2018
  - **Topics**: Lasso, Trees, Forests, IV, DML, Heterogeneous Treatment Effects, Policy Learning

## Notes

- [Causal Inference: A Statistical Learning Approach](https://web.stanford.edu/~swager/causal_inf_book.pdf)
  - **Author**: Stefan Wager (Stanford)
  - **Year**: 2024
  - **Topics**: Neyman-Rubin Model, ATE & CATE Estimation, Regression Forests, Observational Studies, Policy Learning

- [STATS 361: Causal Inference](https://web.stanford.edu/~swager/stats361.pdf)
  - **Author**: Stefan Wager (Stanford)
  - **Year**: 2022
  - **Topics**: Semiparametric Efficiency, DML, Instrumental Variables, Regression Discontinuity, Synthetic Control

- [Causal Inference: The Mixtape](https://mixtape.scunning.com/)
  - **Author**: Scott Cunningham (Baylor)
  - **Year**: 2021
  - **Topics**: Potential Outcomes, DAGs, Matching, IV, Panel Data, Differences-in-Differences, Regression Discontinuity, Synthetic Control

- [Introduction to Causal Inference (Course Notes)](https://www.bradyneal.com/Introduction_to_Causal_Inference-Dec17_2020-Neal.pdf)
  - **Author**: Brady Neal (Mila / McGill)
  - **Year**: 2020
  - **Topics**: Potential Outcomes, SCMs, Identification, Estimation, Unobserved Confounding
