## Video Lectures 

- [Fundamentals of Statistics](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLUl4u3cNGP60uVBMaoNERc6knT_MgPKS0)
  - **Author**: Philippe Rigollet (MIT) 
  - **Year**: 2016 
  - **Lectures**: 22 x 1h:20 [[Material](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-650-statistics-for-applications-fall-2016/)]
  - **Topics**: Inference, MLE, Hypothesis Testing, Regression, Bayesian Statistics, PCA, GLM

- [Statistical Rethinking](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLDcUM9US4XdNOlqSyhe38US8mFgmqzI14)
  - **Author**: Richard McEireath (MPI-EVA Leipzig)
  - **Year**: Yearly 
  - **Lectures**: 20 x 1h:00 [[Material](https://github.com/rmcelreath/stat_rethinking_2026)]
  - **Topics**: Multilevel Models, Confounders, Social Networks, Bad Controls, Gaussian Processes, MCMC, Missing Values, GLM, Sensitivity Analysis, Hidden Markov Models
